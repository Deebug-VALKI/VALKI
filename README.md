## 📖 Overview

<img align="right" width="42%" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/cdb08384-73d9-4557-b715-d2178db2dbe2" alt="VALKI Banner"/>

**VALKI** is a sleek, high-performance mobile companion developed by **DEEBUG** for the global VALORANT community. It bridges the gap between your PC and your daily life, letting you stay synced with your stats, skins, and strategy — anytime, anywhere.

Whether you're checking your **Daily Store** rotation on your commute or analyzing your **Match History** after an intense session, VALKI gives you the insights you need to climb the ranks.

> [!NOTE]
> **VALORANT** is a PC-only title. **VALKI** is a third-party mobile app designed to enhance your experience through data visualization and remote tools.

## 🔒 Security & Disclaimer

We take your account security seriously. A few things to keep in mind:

- **Official Status** — VALKI is not affiliated with, endorsed, or sponsored by Riot Games.
- **API Usage** — Features like Store data use non-public API endpoints, which carries risks common to third-party tools.
- **Privacy First** — We strongly recommend enabling **2FA** on your Riot account before using any third-party service.
